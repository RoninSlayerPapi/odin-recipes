Lasagna

Description

Simple and easy to make lasagna recipe

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs:Eggs make the cheese layer extra creamy.Plus they act as a binding agent(they hold the layer together)

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
